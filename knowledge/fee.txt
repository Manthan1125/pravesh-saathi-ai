University Institute of Engineering and Technology (UIET)
The University Institute of Engineering and Technology (UIET) is the engineering institute of Panjab University, Chandigarh. It offers undergraduate (BE/B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech), and doctoral programs. UIET is known for its strong focus on academics, research, and industry interaction. It is recognized by the AICTE and accredited by the NBA.
Key Features
Courses
UIET offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses, with B.Tech being the flagship program.
Rankings
It has been ranked among the top 25 engineering institutions in India by NIRF 2023.
Placements
UIET has a strong placement record, with the highest package reaching INR 42 LPA and an average package of INR 9.11 LPA in 2024.
Infrastructure
UIET has well-equipped academic blocks with labs, offices, a library, and lecture halls. It also has dedicated blocks for mechanical labs and workshops.
Industry Collaboration
The institute has collaborations and MoUs with industry giants like Infosys (Campus Connect Program), IBM, and Bharti Group.
Research
UIET encourages research activities and has received grants from the Government of India, including TEQIP-II and TEQIP-III.
Student Life
UIET provides various facilities for students, including hostels, sports facilities (gymnasium, swimming pool, athletic tracks, basketball and volleyball courts), and a library.
Location
UIET is located in the southern campus of Panjab University, Sector 25, Chandigarh.
Recognition
UIET is recognized by the AICTE and accredited by the NBA.
